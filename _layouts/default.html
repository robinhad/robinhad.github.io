Yurii Paniv

I'm a PhD student in Computer Science at the Ukrainian Catholic University (UCU), working on developing unsupervised and semi-supervised methods to obtain high-quality machine learning models using minimal data.
My goal is to bring state-of-the-art performance to mid- and low-resource languages across different modalities: text, speech, and vision.

This is important for languages that don't have the same amount of data available as for English, bringing those communities equal economic opportunities.
